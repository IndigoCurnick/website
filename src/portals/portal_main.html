Here is a list of my portals. These portals are homepages or collections of related content that I find interesting

Economics Portal
Economically, I mostly study the history of economics. Not the history of economic thought but rather the economies of nations in the past. My perspective on economics has been formed mostly by the greatest economist of all time: Ludwig von Mises.
History Portal
My interests in history are quite varied, but here I mostly focus on historiography rather than any particular historical event as with the Anglo-Dutch portal.
Philosophy Portal
Philosophically, I have mostly studied epistemology, and considered the capacity for what it is possible to know.
Politics Portal
Politically speaking I mostly follow the Machiavellian school of political analysis based on understanding power and its applications.
Science, Maths, Programming and Technology Portal
I studied a masters in physics at the University of Sussex, specialising in neutrino physics. Since then I have remained interested in all aspects of science
Urban Planning Portal
For a long time I have been concerned with urban planning. I firmly believe that the post-war consensus planning of the British state was an abject failure; we should seek alternatives to this drudgery in our cities and towns.
