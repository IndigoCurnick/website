This is the Economic portal. Economics is broadly the study of the distribution of scarce resources with alternative uses. My views on economics have been informed by the brilliant work of Ludwig von Mises, and his intellectual descendants.

Why We Became Rich

This is probably my most successful essay I have ever written, it has thousands of views (which I am quite proud of). It is based mostly on the Bourgeois series of books by Deirdre McCloskey, some work of Ayn Rand's, and some work of Mises. It also contained a warning about covid and supply chains (which, came pretty much true exactly as I foretold). There must be at least some merit in the works of the economists. Although, I was a little younger when I wrote this, and might write a new version soon that is in a better style.

The Ethics and Economics of Private Property

The Ethics and Economics of Private Property by Hans-Hermann Hoppe. It is easily one of the most stunning economic works I have ever read. From pure first principles Hoppe derives the entire framework of all economic activity. His signature writing style is always a joy to behold to boot. There is no doubt that this work has influenced my own views on economics to the highest pitch.
